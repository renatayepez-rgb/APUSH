MASTER PROMPT FOR CURSOR
App Overview

Build a cross-platform native mobile app (iOS, Android, Web) using Expo + React Native that helps students master AP U.S. History.
The app should cover all APUSH units, provide practice questions, essay feedback, study strategies, and AI-powered study tools (concept maps, flashcards, essay corrections, and study schedules).
The app must be simple, student-friendly, bilingual (English / Spanish), and optimized for AP exam success.

Core Goals

Teach all AP U.S. History units (1–9) clearly

Improve DBQ, LEQ, and SAQ writing

Improve multiple-choice strategy and reading analysis

Provide personalized AI-powered studying

Support test planning, reminders, and notifications

Be deployable to Vercel and Expo-ready
